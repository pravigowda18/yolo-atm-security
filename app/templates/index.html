An innovative solution to enhance the security of Automated Teller Machines by identifying individuals concealing their identity.

Problem Statement

Thefts and fraudulent activities at ATMs are a significant concern for financial institutions and customers alike. Criminals often exploit the anonymity provided by wearing helmets or masks to avoid identification by security cameras. This makes it challenging for law enforcement to track perpetrators and for banks to prevent unauthorized transactions, leading to financial losses and a decline in customer trust.

Objective

Real-time Alert System

Instantly detect a person wearing a mask or helmet as they enter the ATM premises and trigger an immediate alert to the central security system and local authorities.

Transaction Blocking

Temporarily disable the ATM's functionality or block transaction initiation if a concealed face is detected, preventing potential fraud before it happens.

Evidence Capture

Automatically capture high-resolution images and video clips of the suspect, which can be used as crucial evidence for investigation purposes.

Audio Warning System

Issue an automated audio warning within the ATM kiosk, requesting the individual to remove their face covering to proceed, acting as a deterrent.
